## **Credits, Payments, and Receipts**

What is a Credit?

-   Credits represent a 1:1 value with the AR token and can be used to upload data to ArDrive.

Can I view my Credits balance on Viewblock or ArConnect?

-   Credits are specific to ArDrive and can only be viewed and used in ArDrive apps.

Which currencies can I pay in?

-   USD with additional currencies being added soon.

Can I get a copy of my receipt?

-   All receipts are provided by Stripe and are sent to those that provide their email during check out.

Can I use Turbo in the Mobile App?

-   Uploading data through the mobile app with Turbo is possible if you have purchased Credits from the Top Up page. **Credits are unavailable for purchase through the mobile app.**

Can I pay with any credit or debit card?

-   We accept all major credit and debit cards. Purchase restrictions may be implemented by your card provider or country of origin.

Do the value of Credits ever change?

-   Credits have the same storage purchasing power as AR.

Do you keep my credit card info?

-   No. ArDrive keeps a record of your public wallet address, the amount purchased and used Credits when purchases occur. All credit card purchases are processed by Stripe, learn more about their policies [here.](https://stripe.com/privacy "https://stripe.com/privacy")

Can I use Credits elsewhere on the permaweb?

-   Credits can be used with any apps on Arweave that accept Turbo.

## **Refunds, Transfer, & Exchanges**

Can I get a refund for my Credit purchase?

-   All sales of Credits are final. When a Credits are purchased, ArDrive holds that balance of AR to cover the cost of data uploaded.

Do Credits expire?

-   Your Credits may expire after 3 years from the date of purchase.

Can I transfer Credits to another wallet?

-   No, Credits are non-transferable.

Can I buy Credits as a gift?

-   Yes. See [Gifting Credits](/help/using-ardrive-apps-getting-started-gifting-credits) for current gift code instructions.

Can I exchange my Credits for AR?

-   No, ArDrive is not a crypto exchange and Credits cannot be returned or exchanged.

Do I get my Credits back if my Turbo upload fails?

-   You will not be charged for any failed uploads. If you believe you have been charged please [contact](/contact) the ArDrive team.

## **Building with Turbo**

Can I use Turbo in my own app?

-   Yes. Visit the [developer docs](https://docs.ar.io/build) for current ArDrive and Turbo integration guidance.

Is Turbo Available in ArDrive-CLI?

-   Yes. Use the current [developer docs](https://docs.ar.io/build) for CLI guidance.
