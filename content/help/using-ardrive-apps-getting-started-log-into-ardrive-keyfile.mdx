### Logging into ArDrive with your keyfile is an easy way to sign into ArDrive web.

1.  Go to [app.ardrive.io](https://app.ardrive.io/ "https://app.ardrive.io") and choose ‘Select a Keyfile’ to proceed.

2.  Locate the keyfile on your computer and select it. [ArDrive encrypts the keyfile securely on the backend](/help/faqs-faq-how-do-keyfile-and-seed-phrase-login-work "/help/faqs-faq-how-do-keyfile-and-seed-phrase-login-work").

3.  Enter your ArDrive password to log in.

Learn more about keyfiles in the FAQ Section.
