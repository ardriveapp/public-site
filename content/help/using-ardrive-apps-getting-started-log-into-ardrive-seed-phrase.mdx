### Logging into ArDrive with your seed phrase is a secure way to log into ArDrive without having to keep a keyfile on your computer or mobile device.

1.  Go to [app.ardrive.io](http://app.ardrive.io/ "http://app.ardrive.io") and select ‘Enter Seed Phrase’ to proceed. ![0dc1ca02-6177-4669-8f77-69b37e8fd33e.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17189833020571)
    
2.  Enter the 12 word seed phrase in the order it was given seperated by a space. When entered correctly, ArDrive generates the keyfile securely on the backend keeping it encrypted within the app.  
    ![4205306d-ad34-42a5-a85b-af08b1b0b24a.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17189861853723)
    
3.   Enter your ArDrive password to log in.

Learn more about Seed Phrases in the FAQ Section.
