Whether you are creating NFTs on Ethereum, Solana, or Tezos, storing your NFT data and metadata with ArDrive means you do not have to worry about it disappearing. 

ArDrive makes your art & metadata fault tolerant and tamper proof.

Storing your NFT data on ArDrive is a simple and straightforward process.

_At this point, we’re assuming that you have already created an account and are logged in to ArDrive. If not, get started with our_ [_Create a Wallet_](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/en-us/articles/5258344620955-Create-an-Arweave-Wallet) _article first._

## **Working with NFT marketplaces**

Now you have an NFT on the Arweave blockweave where both the transaction and image will last permanently.

You can now upload your NFT to other popular NFT marketplaces like [OpenSea](https://opensea.io/) with confidence that the NFT that you are selling will be around forever!

When listing your NFT be sure that you share your Arweave link with your file’s Data Transaction ID, eg `https://arweave.net/4OIZ1pAi3KZvEnZZ1af15BIuL3Y_ql32WWIrTpQc69Y`

This is sometimes referenced as

`ar://4OIZ1pAi3KZvEnZZ1af15BIuL3Y_ql32WWIrTpQc69Y`
