-   I didn't get my seed phrase from ArDrive, can I still use it to login to ArDrive?
    -   If you have a seed phrase for an existing AR compatible wallet, such as Arweave or ArConnect, it can be used to login to ArDrive.

-   Can I get a seed phrase for my existing wallet?
    
    -   If you did not get a seed phrase when your wallet was created, a seed phrase cannot be generated for you now.  
          
        
-   What is a seed phrase?
    
    -   A seed phrase is a cluster of random words generated by your digital wallet when setting it up, which can be used to login or as an emergency backup to gain access to your account.  
          
        
-   What do I do if I lose my seed phrase?
    
    -   You can still login to the app if you have your keyfile. However, if you lose your seed phrase it cannot be recovered, please make sure to keep it safe.   
          
        
-   How do I keep my seed phrase safe?
    
    -   We recommend writing it down on a piece of paper and safely storing it OR using a password manager.  
          
        
-   Is logging in with a seed phrase safe?
    
    -   Yes, but always verify you are on [ardrive.io](http://ardrive.io/ "http://ardrive.io") or one of the [official ArDrive dapps](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/en-us/articles/15362434821147) when logging in so you are not inadvertently giving your seed phrase into another app.  
          
        
-   Why is my wallet generated every time I login with a seed phrase?
    
    -   The seed phrase identifies and generates your wallet when you login to ArDrive. Your wallet is required to access your drives, upload and sign for transactions, and get your wallet balance.  
          
        
-   What format does my seed phrase need to be in to log into ArDrive?
    
    -   Enter the 12 words as they were given to you, separated by a space.  
          
        
-   Can I use my ArDrive generated wallet in ArConnect?
    
    -   Yes. A wallet generated by ArDrive will allow you to use other apps in the Permaweb ecosystem.  
          
        
-   Can I create a wallet through the mobile app?
    
    -   Yes.  
          
        
-   Can I login with a seed phrase on mobile?
    
    -   Yes.
