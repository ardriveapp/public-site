## How to Gift Credits

You can send Credits through the Gifting App at [https://gift.ardrive.io/](https://gift.ardrive.io//). You can also find the direct link to the Gifting App on the ArDrive website or in the ArDrive App under the gift box icon. 

1.  Select or input the USD value you want to spend.
2.  Provide the gift recipient's email address.
3.  Add a personalized note (optional).
4.  Confirm the information and proceed to the payment page, where you will provide your payment information.

To better understand the purchasing power of Credits, check out our [Pricing Calculator](https://ardrive.io/pricing/).

## Redeeming Gifts

Gift codes can be redeemed in the ArDrive App or the Redemption App. New users should first create a new account/wallet before they can redeem their gift code.

**To redeem in ArDrive:**

1.  Login to [ArDrive](http://app.ardrive.io/ "http://app.ardrive.io") or create a new account [here](https://app.ardrive.io/#/get-started "https://app.ardrive.io/#/get-started"). 
    
2.  Click on the gift icon in the right corner and select “Redeem gift”.
    
3.  Enter the email address of the gift recipient.
    
4.  Enter the gift code provided.
    
5.  Click on "Confirm".

The funds should appear in the recipient's wallet, visible in the Profile menu shortly after redemption.

**In the Redemption App:**

1.  Go to the [Redemption App](https://gift.ardrive.io/redeem "https://gift.ardrive.io/redeem").
    
2.  Specify the wallet address where you want the funds to go.
    
3.  Enter the gift code provided.
    
4.  Provide the email address of the gift recipient.
    
5.  Click on "Redeem".

The funds should appear in the recipient's wallet visible in the Profile menu of the [ArDrive App](https://app.ardrive.io) shortly after redemption.
