The first thing to know about using ArDrive is that the big red **+NEW** button in the top left corner is your best friend.

The **+NEW** button is the control center of the app, where you control your Drive:

-   [Create New Drives](/help/using-ardrive-apps-using-ardrive-create-new-drive/)

-   [Create New Folders](/help/using-ardrive-apps-using-ardrive-new-folders/)

-   [Attach a Drive](/help/using-ardrive-apps-using-ardrive-attach-a-drive/) made by someone else

-   [Upload Files](/help/using-ardrive-apps-using-ardrive-uploading-files/)

-   [Create a manifest](/help/using-ardrive-apps-using-ardrive-create-an-arweave-manifest)
-   Create a Snapshot
