ArDrive is born & bred on the Permaweb and the app exists in a fully decentralized way on Arweave.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="w9Yi-ara1o" />

You can find the latest permanent version of the ArDrive web dApp at [https://dapp.ardrive.io](https://dapp.ardrive.io).

For everyday use, we recommend starting from [https://app.ardrive.io](https://app.ardrive.io). Use the permaweb dApp entry point when you specifically want to access the permanent app deployment.
