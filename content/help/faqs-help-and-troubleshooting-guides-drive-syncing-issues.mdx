If your drive is taking longer than expected to sync, it is usually caused by intermittent gateway indexing delays.

ArDrive relies on Arweave gateways to index newly uploaded files, folders, and drive updates. During periods of heavy network activity or temporary gateway delays, your data may take longer to appear or sync across the app.

In most cases, this resolves on its own within a few hours.

# What to do

- Wait a few hours and check your drive again.

- Refresh ArDrive or sign out and back in if the app still looks stale.

- Advanced users can try changing the data gateway and GraphQL gateway. See [Change Gateway URL For ArDrive](/help/faqs-help-and-troubleshooting-guides-change-gateway-url-for-ardrive).

- If the problem lasts longer than a few hours, check the [ArDrive Discord](https://discord.com/invite/ya4hf2H) for any extended outages or service updates.

- If there is no known outage and your drive still is not syncing, reach out to [support@ardrive.io](mailto:support@ardrive.io) or use the [contact page](/contact).
