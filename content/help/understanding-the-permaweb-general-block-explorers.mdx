There are two block explorers that can be used to view details about all of the transactions, wallets, and Smartweave contracts on Arweave.

-   [ViewBlock](https://viewblock.io/arweave)

-   [https://scar.vercel.app/#/](https://scar.vercel.app/#/)

For more info, check out this video about [how to use Viewblock.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SWyLTcCwcLI)
