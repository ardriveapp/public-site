### **Can you hide a folder or file?**

Yes, hiding files or folders is possible with ArDrive.

**NOTE: Drives cannot be hidden at this time**

To hide a file or folder press the ‘three dots’ to the right of the file or folder, which will reveal the option to hide that particular file or folder.

Click on ‘Hide’ and that folder or file will be hidden from view.

### Can you unhide files or folders?

If you want to unhide the files or folders that you have hidden, go to the ‘three dots’ to the right of Drive box and hit the ‘Reveal hidden items’ option.

To hide these items again press the same ‘Three Dots’ and hit ‘Conceal hidden items’.
