## Using ArDrive requires an Arweave Wallet and creating a wallet is easy with ArDrive.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="kUaCpRXItss" />

1.  Visit [app.ardrive.io](app.ardrive.io) and click **“New User? Get Started here”.**
    

![deefb9b0-d986-4f54-a00f-05379679faaa.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17190434431771)

2.  We will walk you through a few things that may be new to you compared to other apps in the signup process. ![a7746989-1620-469a-b9ec-2796b7e276d5.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17190395927707)
    
3.  A 12 word seed phrase and keyfile are generated with the option to download the keyfile. ![c46e1c67-5d13-431f-9354-afbbb2883a19.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17190441126171)
    
4.  Write down the seed phrase & store it in a secure location.
    
5.  Confirm the seed phrase by providing the words ArDrive asks for in order to download the keyfile. ![5bec5ff3-0b91-4e33-9abe-1258faa95c99.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17190451378843) 
    
6.  Download your keyfile and keep it in a safe place. ![5423cc83-73e2-4083-ab3b-da59eee83224.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17190424708251) 
    
7.  Create and confirm a password that you will use with your seed phrase or keyfile if you create a private Drive.  
    ![a357be5a-e689-4dca-ae25-ad9a422f092a.png](https://help.ardrive.io/hc/article_attachments/17190412637595)  
      
    **NOTE:** The seed phrase cannot be recovered by anybody. The keyfile can be recovered by using the seed phrase in ArDrive or another wallet service like ArConnect.
