## Using ArDrive requires an Arweave Wallet and creating a wallet is easy with ArDrive.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="kUaCpRXItss" />

1.  Visit [app.ardrive.io](https://app.ardrive.io) and click **“New User? Get Started here”.**

2.  We will walk you through a few things that may be new to you compared to other apps in the signup process.

3.  A 12 word seed phrase and keyfile are generated with the option to download the keyfile.

4.  Write down the seed phrase & store it in a secure location.

5.  Confirm the seed phrase by providing the words ArDrive asks for in order to download the keyfile.

6.  Download your keyfile and keep it in a safe place.

7.  Create and confirm a password that you will use with your seed phrase or keyfile if you create a private Drive.

    **NOTE:** The seed phrase cannot be recovered by anybody. The keyfile can be recovered by using the seed phrase in ArDrive or another wallet service like Wander.
