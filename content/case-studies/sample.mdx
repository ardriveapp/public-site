### ArDrive: Arweave’s OG App

Since the early days of Arweave, [**ArDrive**](ardrive.io) **has been the flagship app for permanent storage**, showing what’s possible when data is truly **decentralized, tamper-proof, and available forever**.  
  

From individuals to enterprises, **ArDrive has set the standard for what permanent storage should be:**

*   **Giving everyone access to permanent storage** \- just not developers
    
*   **Allowing people to upload any type of file to Arweave**
    
*   **Providing a simple and intuitive experience** based around a familiar file/folder structure
    

  
Now, with **ar.io, ArDrive becomes even more powerful**—expanding how, where, and why permanent storage can be used.  
  

![](/case-studies/ardrive/img-3a786e40.png)

#### The Problems ArDrive has Already Solved

  
For years, we have become accustomed to cloud storage from Dropbox, Google, and Apple. While powerful and convenient, this **data storage comes with built-in limitations**:  
  

🔹 **Lost files** – If a cloud provider shuts down, gets acquired, or decides to delete inactive accounts, your data can disappear overnight. Just look at [Adobe’s Creative Cloud](https://helpx.adobe.com/ca/creative-cloud/kb/eol-creative-cloud-synced-files.html).  
  

🔹 **Subscription-based access** – With traditional storage, **you’re renting space, not owning it**. The second you stop paying, your files are at risk.  
  

🔹 **Centralized control** – A handful of companies manage **nearly all cloud storage**, meaning your data is subject to **policy changes, restrictions, and access limitations**.  
  

🔹 **Limited longevity** – Data stored today **isn’t guaranteed to be there tomorrow**, leaving businesses, creators, and individuals with **no long-term security**.

  
ArDrive changed that. **For the first time, users could store data permanently, knowing it would be accessible no matter what.**

  

![](/case-studies/ardrive/img-5b962e3f.jpg)

#### Storage as it should be

  
With **ArDrive** your files are stored **permanently on Arweave’s blockchain technology**. That means:  
  

✅ **You pay once, and your files stay online forever**—no subscriptions, no renewals.  
✅ **No company or platform can delete your data**—you own it, and only you control access.  
✅ **Your files are always available**—even if ArDrive or any single gateway disappears.

**  
It’s like putting your files in a vault that can never be lost, stolen, or shut down.**

  
This is what cloud storage **should** have been from the start - **permanent, private, and truly yours.**

  
But **ArDrive alone was just the first step** - there is more to this journey. That’s where **ar.io comes in**.

  

#### How ar.io Accelerates ArDrive

ArDrive set the foundation, but **ar.io makes permanent storage more accessible, flexible, and usable across a wider range of applications**.

  
  

##### 🔹 ArNS Integration – Name Your Storage

*   Instead of sharing **long transaction IDs**, ArDrive users can assign **human-readable ArNS names** to files, making them easier to find, organize, and share. You can give your file a unique name like "spainholiday" or an [undername](https://docs.ar.io/learn/arns#name-registration) such as “spain\_holidayvideo” instead of “3gd5fUVEcCqhDibQv8JKJ9Q9qZReLcJbvh03c17ZiZe”  
      
    

![](/case-studies/ardrive/img-09c097c1.jpg)

##### 🔹 Decentralized Gateway Access – No Single Point of Failure

*   Instead of relying on **a single provider**, ArDrive users now benefit from **ar.io’s network of independent gateways**, ensuring **files stay accessible even if one gateway goes offline**.
    

  
  

##### 🔹 Turbo for Faster, More Efficient Uploads

*   **Turbo-powered bundling and indexing** streamlines **large file uploads**, reducing the time and cost of storing data permanently.
    
*   **Easier payments** – Users can now **pay for storage with traditional currencies or other cryptocurrencies not just the Arweave Token**
    

  

  

##### 🔹 Developer & Enterprise-Grade Tools

*   **Bulk uploads, automated workflows, and programmatic storage management** are now easier with **ArDrive’s CLI and API tools**, enhanced by **ar.io’s infrastructure**.  
      
    

Together, **ArDrive and ar.io make permanent storage not just possible—but practical** and highly decentralized.
