Ar.io provides long-term file storage designed for data that must remain accessible and intact over time. Documents, media, and archives are stored using decentralized infrastructure that eliminates single points of failure and prevents files from being altered, encrypted, or deleted after they are written.

This makes ar.io suitable for safeguarding mission-critical data — including legal records, business documents, research materials, and historical archives — where loss of access is not an option.

### Why ar.io for enterprise file storage?

Most enterprise data is not lost because it is destroyed. It becomes unavailable because systems fail, infrastructure is compromised, or access is blocked elsewhere.

Ar.io is designed to ensure that once files are stored, access to them is preserved — regardless of outages, ransomware incidents, vendor changes, or operational disruption.

### What ar.io enables

**Ransomware-resilient data access**  
Files stored via ar.io cannot be modified, encrypted, or overwritten after they are written. This ensures that even in the event of a ransomware attack or internal compromise, clean, original versions of data remain accessible.

**Guaranteed long-term access**  
Ar.io separates file access from individual systems, contracts, or providers. This ensures that files remain reachable even if surrounding infrastructure, services, or organizations change over time.

**Immutable records for compliance and trust**  
Once stored, files remain exactly as they were at the time of creation. This immutability supports compliance, audits, legal defensibility, and long-term trust in records that must remain unchanged.

**Scalable storage for large and evolving datasets**  
Ar.io supports efficient ingestion of both small files and large datasets through optimized batching and upload workflows. Teams can scale storage volumes without compromising reliability or access guarantees.

**Operational flexibility and integration**  
Ar.io provides programmatic interfaces and tooling that integrate into existing workflows, automation pipelines, and data management systems — allowing organizations to adopt permanent storage without disrupting operations.

**Resilient access without single points of failure**  
Files are served through a decentralized access layer operated as part of ar.io's infrastructure. This architecture ensures continued availability even if individual systems or access paths are disrupted.
